📈 Reliance Equity Stock Market Analysis using Business Analytics
About the Project

This project explores historical stock market data for Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) using Business Analytics techniques to uncover trends, patterns, and insights that can support investment and trading decisions.

Using Python and data analytics libraries, the study examines stock price behaviour, trading volume, volatility, and price relationships over a two-year period. The analysis combines Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), statistical modelling, time-series techniques, and predictive analytics to better understand market dynamics and investor behaviour.

Key Objectives
Analyse historical stock price trends (Open, High, Low, Close)
Investigate the relationship between trading volume and price movements
Identify significant peaks and troughs in stock performance
Measure stock volatility using Bollinger Bands
Explore moving averages to identify short, medium, and long-term trends
Build a predictive model to estimate closing prices based on trading activity

Key Insights
Strong positive correlations were observed among Open, High, Low, and Close prices.
Trading volume exhibited a weak negative correlation with stock prices.
Moving averages successfully highlighted trend reversals and long-term market direction.
Bollinger Bands identified periods of heightened volatility and potential trading opportunities.
The predictive regression model achieved an R² score of approximately 0.81, demonstrating strong explanatory power for stock price movements.
Business Value

This project demonstrates how data analytics can transform raw financial data into actionable insights for investors, analysts, and decision-makers. It showcases practical applications of data visualisation, statistical analysis, and predictive modelling within financial markets.
